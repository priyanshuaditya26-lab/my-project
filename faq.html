General Questions

How do I submit a research proposal?

Use the NHREC portal to submit proposals. Register an account, complete the application, and upload required documents.

How long does review take?

Timelines vary depending on the study type. Check the Process Map in Resources for estimated review times.

Who needs ethical approval?

All research involving human participants requires ethical approval before recruitment or data collection.
