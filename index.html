About NHREC

The National Research Ethics Committee of India (NRECOI) was affiliated by the research Ministry of India, to ensure that all research-related research involving human participants meets the highest ethical standards. The Committee provides oversight, accreditation, and capacity building for Institutional Ethics Committees nationwide.
