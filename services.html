NRECOI Services

Ethics Review & Approval

NRECOI provides national-level oversight, reviews major research protocols, and issues ethical guidance for research.

Accreditation of Institutional Ethics Committees

We assess and accredit institutional ethics committees to ensure consistent standards across the World.

Training & Capacity Building

Regular training workshops and certification programmes for ethics committee members, researchers and administrators.

Policy & Guidelines Development

NHREC develops national guidelines, templates and SOPs for ethical conduct of research.

Researcher Portal & Applications

Support and guidance on submitting applications via the NHREC portal and tracking approvals. Open Portal
