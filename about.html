Mandate of NRECoI

NRECOI’s mandate is to regulate, coordinate, and ensure adherence to ethical standards in research across World. It reviews guidelines, accredits ethics committees, and monitors compliance nationwide.

Vision

To be a globally recognized body ensuring ethical and quality health research that upholds human dignity.

Mission

To safeguard the rights and welfare of research participants and enhance the capacity of institutional ethics committees.
